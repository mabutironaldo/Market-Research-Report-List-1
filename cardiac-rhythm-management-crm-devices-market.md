<p><p>Cardiac Rhythm Management (CRM) devices are used to manage and treat heart rhythm disorders. Pacemakers are small devices implanted in the chest to control abnormal heart rhythms by monitoring and regulating heartbeats. Implantable Cardioverter Defibrillators (ICDs) continuously monitor heart rhythms and deliver electric shocks to restore normal rhythm if needed. Cardiac Resynchronization Therapy (CRT) devices, including CRT-P and CRT-D, coordinate heart contractions in patients with heart failure. These devices improve the heart's efficiency and quality of life. Collectively, these CRM devices play a crucial role in maintaining the normal rhythm and functioning of the heart.</p></p>
