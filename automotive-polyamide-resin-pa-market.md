<p><strong>The Automotive Polyamide Resin (PA) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Reactive Type</li><li>Nonreactive Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive polyamide resin market is divided into two types: reactive type and nonreactive type. </p><p>Reactive type polyamide resin is a thermosetting material that undergoes a chemical reaction during the curing process, resulting in a strong and durable final product. It is commonly used in applications that require high resistance to heat, chemicals, and abrasion, such as engine parts or electrical components. </p><p>Nonreactive type polyamide resin, on the other hand, is a thermoplastic material that can be melted and reshaped multiple times without undergoing any chemical reaction. It is commonly used in applications that require flexibility and ease of processing, such as interior parts or exterior trims.</p></p>
