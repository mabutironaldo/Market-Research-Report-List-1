<p><p>The commercial airport full body scanners market consists of different types of scanners, including optical scanners, laser scanners, structured light scanners, and others. Optical scanners utilize cameras to capture images of the body for screening. Laser scanners use laser beams to create 3D representations of the body. Structured light scanners project a pattern onto the body and analyze the distortion to create a 3D image. Other types of scanners may include biometric scanners or millimeter-wave scanners. These different types of scanners offer varying levels of accuracy, speed, and privacy considerations for security purposes at airports.</p></p>
