<p><p>Graphene flakes are a form of graphene, a one-atom-thick sheet of carbon. The Graphene Flakes market can be classified into two types: Single-Layer Graphene Flakes and Bilayer Graphene Flakes. Single-Layer Graphene Flakes consist of individual flakes that are only one layer thick, offering unique properties such as flexibility and electrical conductivity. On the other hand, Bilayer Graphene Flakes consist of two layers of graphene stacked on top of each other, which can exhibit different properties compared to single-layer flakes. These classifications help in distinguishing and analyzing the characteristics and potential applications of graphene flakes.</p></p>
