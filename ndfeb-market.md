<p><p>NdFeB, also known as neodymium-iron-boron, is a type of rare earth magnet that is widely used in various industries. There are three main types of NdFeB magnets available in the market. Sintered Nd-Fe-B magnets are produced through a powder metallurgy process, offering high magnetic properties and strong resistance to corrosion. Bonded Nd-Fe-B magnets are made by combining NdFeB powder with a binder, resulting in lower magnetic properties but better mechanical strength. Hot pressed NdFeB magnets are manufactured through a heat treatment process, offering improved coercivity and temperature stability compared to sintered magnets.</p></p>
