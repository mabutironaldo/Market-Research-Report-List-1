<p><p>Commercial air humidifiers are devices used to increase the moisture in the air of large commercial spaces. Two popular types in the market are the vapor type air humidifier and the water spray air humidifier. The vapor type air humidifier works by converting water into steam, which is then released into the air. On the other hand, the water spray air humidifier releases a fine mist of water droplets into the air to increase moisture levels. Both types serve the same purpose of improving air quality and comfort in commercial settings.</p></p>
