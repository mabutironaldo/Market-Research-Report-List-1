<p><strong>The Subsea Cable Tracker Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Tone Detector Subsea Cable Tracker</li><li>Pulse Induction Subsea Cable Tracker</li><li>Mag-gradiometer Subsea Cable Tracker</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The subsea cable tracker market consists of three main types: tone detector subsea cable tracker, pulse induction subsea cable tracker, and mag-gradiometer subsea cable tracker. A tone detector subsea cable tracker uses audio signals to detect and locate underwater cables. A pulse induction subsea cable tracker uses electromagnetic pulses to identify and track the cables. On the other hand, a mag-gradiometer subsea cable tracker measures variations in the Earth's magnetic field to locate and trace subsea cables. These technologies are vital for cable maintenance, repair, and installation in the underwater environment.</p></p>
