<p><p>Polypectomy forceps are a medical tool used to remove polyps from the body. They come in two types: hot and cold. Hot polypectomy forceps use an electrically heated wire loop to cut and cauterize the polyp, preventing bleeding. Cold polypectomy forceps, on the other hand, work solely by applying pressure to the polyp to remove it. The choice between hot and cold forceps depends on the specific case and the preference of the healthcare professional performing the procedure.</p></p>
