<p><strong>The Insulation Monitoring Device Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>AC Type</li><li>DC Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Insulation Monitoring Device (IMD) market can be categorized into two types: AC Type and DC Type. AC Type IMDs are designed to monitor and protect the insulation of electrical systems running on alternating current (AC), commonly found in buildings and industries. They continuously measure the insulation resistance and provide alarms or notifications in case of insulation faults, helping prevent electrical accidents. On the other hand, DC Type IMDs are specifically used for direct current (DC) electrical systems, typically seen in renewable energy installations like solar power plants and battery storage systems. They serve a similar purpose of insulation monitoring to ensure safe and efficient operation.</p></p>
