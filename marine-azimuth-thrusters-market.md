<p><p>Marine azimuth thrusters refer to propulsion systems used in ships and other marine vessels to provide maneuverability. These thrusters are designed to rotate 360 degrees, allowing for omnidirectional thrust, which enables the vessel to move in any direction. Azimuth thrusters are commonly used in various types of ships, including tugboats, offshore supply vessels, and ferries, as they offer excellent maneuverability and control.</p><p>The marine azimuth thrusters market is expected to witness steady growth in the coming years. The increasing demand for commercial vessels, such as tugboats and ferries, coupled with the rise in offshore activities, is driving the market growth.
