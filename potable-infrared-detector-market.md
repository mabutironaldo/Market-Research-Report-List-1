<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>Potable infrared detectors are used for various applications, including surveillance, temperature measurement, and gas detection. There are two main types of potable infrared detectors: active thermal infrared detectors and passive thermal infrared detectors.</p><p>Active thermal infrared detectors emit their own infrared radiation and measure the reflected or emitted energy to detect objects. This technology is commonly used in distance measurement devices like rangefinders and for presence detection.</p><p>Passive thermal infrared detectors, on the other hand, detect the natural infrared radiation emitted by objects without emitting their own. This technology is widely used in night vision devices, security cameras, and fire detection systems.</p><p>Both types of potable infrared detectors have their own advantages and applications, depending on the specific requirements of the user.</p></p>
