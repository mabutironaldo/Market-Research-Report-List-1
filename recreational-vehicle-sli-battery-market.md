Maintenance-free type batteries, on the other hand, do not require any regular maintenance as they are sealed and have special designs to resist drying. </p><p>The "others" category includes advanced battery technologies like absorbent glass mat (AGM) batteries, which are spill-proof and provide enhanced performance, and lithium-ion batteries, which are lightweight and offer high energy storage.</p></p>
