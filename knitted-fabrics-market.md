<p><p>The knitted fabrics market consists of two main types: weft knitted fabrics and warp knitted fabrics. Weft knitted fabrics are created by using a single yarn that loops back and forth horizontally, resulting in a flexible and stretchable fabric. On the other hand, warp knitted fabrics are formed by interlocking multiple yarns vertically, resulting in a more stable and durable fabric. Both types of knitted fabrics offer a wide range of applications in the textile industry, including clothing, home textiles, and industrial textiles.</p></p>
