<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>Collapsible Electric Power Steering (EPS) systems are classified into three types based on their structure and function. The Rack Assist Type (REPS) EPS uses a motor and gear mechanism installed on the steering rack to provide assistance to the driver. The Column Assist Type (CEPS) EPS, on the other hand, integrates the assistance motor and gear mechanism into the steering column. Lastly, the Pinion Assist Type (PEPS) EPS utilizes a motor and gear mechanism attached to the pinion gear, which directly assists the steering mechanism. These types differ in their design and placement but serve the common purpose of enhancing steering control and ease for the driver.</p></p>
