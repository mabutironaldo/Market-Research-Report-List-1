<p><p>Automated Optical Inspection (AOI) Systems are used in the manufacturing industry for quality control and defect detection. There are two types of AOI systems in the market - 2D AOI Systems and 3D AOI Systems. </p><p>2D AOI Systems use cameras to capture images of the printed circuit board (PCB) and analyze them based on predefined parameters. This system is commonly used for inspecting defects on the surface of the PCB.</p><p>On the other hand, 3D AOI Systems utilize advanced technologies such as lasers or structured light to measure and capture the height and shape of components on the PCB. This enables comprehensive inspection of both the surface and solder joints, providing a more accurate analysis of defects.</p></p>
