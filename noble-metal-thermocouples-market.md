<p><p>Noble metal thermocouples are temperature sensors that consist of two dissimilar noble metal wires. Three common types of noble metal thermocouples are R type, S type, and B type. The R type thermocouple is made of platinum and rhodium wires, the S type uses platinum and rhodium wires, and the B type is composed of platinum and rhodium-palladium wires. Each type has different temperature ranges and sensitivities, making them suitable for various applications in industries like petrochemicals, power plants, and aerospace.</p></p>
