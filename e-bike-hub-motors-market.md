<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>E-Bike hub motors are classified into two main types: rear motor and front motor. Rear hub motors are positioned at the back of the bicycle, near the rear wheel. They provide better traction and stability, making them suitable for off-road or hilly terrains. Front hub motors are placed in the front wheel, offering a more balanced weight distribution and easier maneuverability. They are preferred for urban commuting or leisurely rides. The choice between rear and front hub motors depends on individual preferences, terrain conditions, and desired biking experience.</p></p>
