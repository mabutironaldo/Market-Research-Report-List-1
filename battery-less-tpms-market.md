<p><p>Battery-less TPMS (Tire Pressure Monitoring System) is a wireless system that constantly monitors the air pressure and temperature inside tires without the need for a battery. It utilizes sensors that absorb energy from the motion of the tire, converting it into electrical energy to power the system.</p><p>The market for battery-less TPMS is expected to witness significant growth in the coming years. The increasing focus on improving vehicle safety and reducing fuel consumption is driving the demand for advanced tire pressure monitoring systems. Additionally, strict government regulations regarding vehicle safety and the need to reduce carbon emissions are further propelling the market growth.</p><p>The growing adoption of electric vehicles (EVs) is also positively impacting the battery-less TPMS market. EVs require efficient tire pressure monitoring systems to optimize fuel efficiency and extend the driving range. Moreover, the development of smart cities and the integration of advanced technologies in vehicles are expected to drive the demand for battery-less TPMS.</p><p>In terms of technology, the market can be divided into radio frequency (RF) and acoustic wave-based systems. RF-based systems dominate the market due to their reliability and accuracy in monitoring tire pressure. However, the acoustic wave-based systems are expected to witness significant growth owing to their ability to accurately measure tire pressure, temperature, and acceleration.</p><p>Geographically, North America and Europe are the largest markets for battery-less TPMS.
