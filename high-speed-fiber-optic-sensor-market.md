<p><p>The high speed fiber optic sensor market can be divided into two types: Point Fiber Optic Sensors (FOS) and Distributed Fiber Optic Sensors. Point FOS consists of individual sensors that provide measurements at specific points along the fiber optic cable. These sensors are commonly used in applications where localized measurements are required. On the other hand, Distributed FOS uses a continuous length of fiber optic cable to provide measurements along its entire length. This type of sensor is suitable for applications that require monitoring over large areas or structures. Both types of sensors play a crucial role in various industries, including aerospace, automotive, and healthcare.</p></p>
