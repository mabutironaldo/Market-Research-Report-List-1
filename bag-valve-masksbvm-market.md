<p><p>Bag Valve Masks (BVMs) are medical devices used to provide positive pressure ventilation to patients who are unable to breathe on their own or require assistance in breathing. They consist of a self-inflating bag attached to a face mask, with a valve system that allows for the delivery of oxygen or a mixture of oxygen and air to the patient. BVMs are commonly used in emergency situations, such as cardiopulmonary resuscitation (CPR) and in anesthesia procedures.</p><p>The Bag Valve Masks (BVM) market is expected to witness significant growth during the forecast period.
