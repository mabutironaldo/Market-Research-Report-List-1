<p><p>Natural Fiber Composite Material refers to a type of material that is composed of natural fibers combined with a polymer matrix. The market for this material is mainly categorized into three types: Carbon Fiber Composite Material, Glass Fiber Composites Material, and Other. Carbon Fiber Composite Material utilizes carbon fibers for its reinforcement, providing high strength and low weight properties. Glass Fiber Composites Material, on the other hand, employs glass fibers for reinforcement, offering excellent electrical insulation and impact resistance. The "Other" category encompasses various natural fibers like hemp, jute, and kenaf that are used for reinforcement purposes in composite materials.</p></p>
