<p><p>The speedboat market is classified into three types based on their propulsion systems. The outboard type speedboat has the engine located outside the hull, offering easy maintenance and maneuverability. The sterndrive type has an inboard engine with a steerable drive unit, providing better fuel efficiency and powerful acceleration. In contrast, the inboard type speedboat has the engine inside the hull, allowing for a larger cabin space and improved stability. These different types cater to the varied preferences and needs of speedboat enthusiasts.</p></p>
