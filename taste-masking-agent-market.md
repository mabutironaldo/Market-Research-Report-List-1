<p><p>Taste masking agents are substances used to cover or mask the bitter, unpleasant taste of certain active pharmaceutical ingredients (APIs) or food ingredients. The aim of taste masking agents is to improve patient compliance by making the medication more palatable and reducing possible adverse reactions.</p><p>The global taste masking agent market is expected to grow at a steady CAGR of 4.2% during the forecast period. The market is primarily driven by the increasing demand for pediatric and geriatric medications, as these patient populations often struggle with medication compliance due to taste issues. Additionally, the rising prevalence of chronic diseases and the growing pharmaceutical industry are also contributing to the market growth.</p><p>One of the latest trends in the taste masking agent market is the development of novel taste masking technologies. Manufacturers are increasingly focusing on innovative methods to efficiently mask the taste of APIs. Such advancements in taste masking technologies are expected to drive the market growth in the coming years.</p><p>Furthermore, the demand for natural taste masking agents is also on the rise.
