<p><p>The PCB In-Circuit Tester market comprises two main types: Semi-Automatic Tester and Fully-Automatic Tester. Semi-Automatic Testers require some human intervention throughout the testing process. They are usually used for low to medium volume production, as they offer a cost-effective solution with the flexibility to adapt to various PCB configurations. On the other hand, Fully-Automatic Testers do not require human intervention and are suitable for high-volume production. These testers provide a faster and more efficient testing process, increasing productivity and reducing labor costs.</p></p>
