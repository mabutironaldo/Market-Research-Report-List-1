<p><strong>The Polyimide Fiber Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Type I</li><li>Type II</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Polyimide fiber is categorized into two types - Type I and Type II. Type I polyimide fiber is derived from orthoaminodiphenyl (oADP) and pyromellitic dianhydride (PMDA). It is mainly used in the aerospace industry for applications such as thermal insulation and composite materials due to its excellent resistance to heat and dimensional stability. On the other hand, Type II polyimide fiber is derived from methoxy-substituted diamines and PMDA. Its superior flexibility and processability make it suitable for applications in electronics, optoelectronics, and other industrial sectors.</p></p>
