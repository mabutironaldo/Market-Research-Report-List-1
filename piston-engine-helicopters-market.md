<p><p>Piston engine helicopters are classified into two main market types: single-engine helicopters and multi-engine helicopters. These helicopters are usually smaller in size and are mainly used for personal and recreational purposes. On the other hand, multi-engine helicopters are equipped with two or more piston engines, ensuring increased redundancy and safety.
