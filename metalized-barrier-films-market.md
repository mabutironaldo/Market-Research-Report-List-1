<p><p>The metalized barrier films market comprises various types of films including polyethylene, BOPT (biaxially oriented polyethylene terephthalate), polypropylene, aluminum, polyamide, PVDC (polyvinylidene chloride), EVOH (ethylene-vinyl alcohol copolymer), and others. Polyamide films offer excellent heat resistance and gas barrier properties. PVDC films are popular for their exceptional oxygen and aroma barrier properties. EVOH films provide excellent gas barrier and chemical resistance. Other films include various specialized materials designed to cater to specific barrier requirements.</p></p>
