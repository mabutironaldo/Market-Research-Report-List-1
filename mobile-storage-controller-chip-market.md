NOR chips are used for booting instructions in devices, while SRAM chips offer fast, low-power storage. EEPROM chips are widely used for small, non-volatile storage in mobile devices. These different types of chips cater to various mobile storage and memory needs, contributing to the overall mobile storage controller chip market.</p></p>
