<p><p>Seal adhesive tape market consists of two main types: single side tape and double side tape. Single side tapes have adhesive applied on only one side, allowing it to stick securely to surfaces when applied. This type of tape is commonly used for packaging and sealing purposes. On the other hand, double side tapes have adhesive applied on both sides, allowing it to adhere two surfaces together. Double side tapes are commonly used for mounting, bonding, and joining applications where a strong and permanent bond is desired.</p></p>
