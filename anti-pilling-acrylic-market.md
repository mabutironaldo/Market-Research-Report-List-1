<p><p>Anti-pilling acrylic refers to a type of acrylic fiber that has been specially treated to prevent the formation of pills or fuzz on the fabric surface. Anti-pilling acrylic is designed to minimize the occurrence of pills, resulting in a smoother and more aesthetically pleasing fabric.</p><p>The market for anti-pilling acrylic has been witnessing significant growth in recent years.
