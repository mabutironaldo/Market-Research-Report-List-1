<p><strong>The Solar Powered Car Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Crystalline Silicon Solar Cells</li><li>Thin-Film Solar Cells</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The solar powered car market is divided into two types: crystalline silicon solar cells and thin-film solar cells. Crystalline silicon solar cells are made using a highly purified form of silicon and are more commonly used in solar panels due to their high efficiency. Thin-film solar cells, on the other hand, are made by depositing a thin layer of photovoltaic material onto a substrate. While thin-film solar cells are less efficient than crystalline silicon cells, they offer flexibility and can be integrated into various surfaces, making them suitable for use in solar powered cars.</p></p>
