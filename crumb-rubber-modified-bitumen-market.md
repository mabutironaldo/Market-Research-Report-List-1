<p><p>Crumb Rubber Modified Bitumen (CRMB) is a type of bituminous binder produced by blending crumb rubber obtained from end-of-life tires with bitumen. <p><strong>The Crumb Rubber Modified Bitumen Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Wet Method</li><li>Dry Method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Crumb Rubber Modified Bitumen (CRMB) market can be categorized into two types - Wet Method and Dry Method. In the Wet Method, the crumb rubber is mixed with bitumen and a chemical agent in a hot process, resulting in a homogenous blend. This method offers better blending and dispersion of rubber particles, leading to improved performance properties. Conversely, the Dry Method involves blending the crumb rubber and bitumen in a dry process, where the rubber particles are coated with bitumen. This method offers economical benefits and improved workability.</p></p>
