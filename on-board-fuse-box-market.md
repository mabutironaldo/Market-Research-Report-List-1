The market for on-board fuse boxes can be categorized into two types: interior and under-hood. The interior fuse box is typically located inside the vehicle, near the driver's cabin, and houses fuses for various interior electrical components such as lights, power windows, and audio systems. On the other hand, the under-hood fuse box is generally placed in the engine compartment and contains fuses for critical systems like the engine, fuel pump, and cooling fans.</p></p>
