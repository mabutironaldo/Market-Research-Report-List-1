<p><p>Expandable paper is a unique type of paper that has the ability to expand in size when exposed to moisture. The rising demand for packaging solutions in various industries, such as food and beverages, cosmetics, and pharmaceuticals, is one of the key factors driving the market. Expandable paper offers advantages like excellent printability, tear resistance, and eco-friendliness, making it an attractive choice for packaging applications.</p><p>In addition, the increasing adoption of expandable paper in the printing industry for applications like book covers, poster printing, and promotional materials is also contributing to market growth. <p><p>The expandable paper market can be classified into two main types: coated and uncoated. Coated paper is treated with a layer of clay or other substances to improve its smoothness and ink absorption capacity. It is commonly used for high-quality printing applications like magazines and brochures. On the other hand, uncoated paper doesn't have a surface coating and has a rougher texture. It is more suitable for applications like newspaper printing and packaging materials. Both types cater to different market demands based on their unique characteristics and applications.</p></p>
