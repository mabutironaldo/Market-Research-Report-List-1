<p><p>The strip seal expansion joint market consists of two main types: strip seal expansion joints with straight head and strip seal expansion joints with bending head. Strip seal expansion joints with straight head have a fixed configuration and are commonly used in applications where there is no requirement for lateral movements. On the other hand, strip seal expansion joints with bending head have a flexible head that allows for lateral movements, making them suitable for applications that involve bridge structures or heavy traffic loads.</p></p>
