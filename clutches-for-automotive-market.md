<p><p>In the automotive market, there are different types of clutches used in vehicles. Manual transmission clutches are commonly found in cars with a traditional gearbox where the driver manually changes gears. Automatic transmission (AT) clutches are used in vehicles with an automatic gearbox, where the transmission shifts gears automatically. Automated Manual Transmission (AMT) clutches combine the benefits of manual and automatic transmissions, offering a manual mode with the convenience of automatic shifting. Continuously Variable Transmission (CVT) clutches use a belt and pulley system to offer seamless gear shifting without discrete gear ratios.</p></p>
