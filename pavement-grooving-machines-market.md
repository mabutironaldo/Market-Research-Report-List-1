<p><strong>The Pavement Grooving Machines Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Type I</li><li>Type II</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Pavement grooving machines are classified into two types: Type I and Type II. Type I machines are designed to groove pavements for enhancing traction, reducing hydroplaning, and improving skid resistance. They feature a single diamond blade setup and are typically used for highway and runway applications. On the other hand, Type II machines are multi-blade groovers that offer flexibility in groove width and depth. They are commonly employed for concrete and asphalt surface grooving, including bridge decks and parking lots, to improve drainage and reduce the risk of cracking.</p></p>
