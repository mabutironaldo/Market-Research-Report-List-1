Hexagon bolts are characterized by their six-sided heads and are commonly used in construction and automotive industries. Cuphead bolts have a rounded head resembling a cup and are used in applications requiring a smooth finish. Coach bolts feature a square or hexagon head and are ideal for timber connections. Countersunk bolts have a flat head that sinks below the surface, creating a flush finish. The market also includes other bolt types catering to specific industry needs.</p></p>
