<p><p>Area scan cameras are widely used in industrial applications for capturing still images or videos of a specific area. There are two major types of area scan cameras in the market - CMOS and CCD. CMOS (Complementary Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor) cameras are popular because they provide lower power consumption, faster readout speeds, and higher pixel densities. CCD (Charge-Coupled Device) cameras, on the other hand, offer better image quality, higher dynamic range, and lower noise levels. Both types cater to different industrial needs, with CMOS cameras being more suitable for tasks that require speed, and CCD cameras excelling in applications demanding high-quality imaging.</p></p>
