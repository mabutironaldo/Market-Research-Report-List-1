<p><p>Powder metal parts are components that are manufactured through a process called powder metallurgy. This process involves the blending of metal powders and then compacting them into a desired shape, typically using a die. Powder metal parts offer various advantages such as cost-effectiveness, complex shape capabilities, and high precision.</p><p>The future outlook of the powder metal parts market looks promising.
