<p><p>OEM coatings market comprises various types of coatings, namely powder coatings, water-borne coatings, solvent-borne coatings, and radiation curable coatings. Powder coatings are dry and durable coatings applied as a free-flowing powder over substrates. Water-borne coatings are applied using water as a solvent, offering low VOC emissions. Solvent-borne coatings use organic solvents for application, providing excellent durability. Radiation curable coatings are cured using ultraviolet light or electron beam, resulting in a cross-linked and cured surface. These coatings find applications in the original equipment manufacturing industry, offering protection and aesthetic appeal to different surfaces.</p></p>
