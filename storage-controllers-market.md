<p><p>Storage controllers are hardware devices or software systems that manage data storage by controlling the flow of data between the computer and the storage devices. The increasing demand for data storage due to the proliferation of digital content, such as videos, social media, and IoT devices, is driving the need for more advanced storage controllers. The growing trend of software-defined storage (SDS) and hyper-converged infrastructure (HCI) is also contributing to the growth of the storage controllers market.</p><p>Furthermore, the increasing adoption of solid-state drives (SSDs) over traditional hard disk drives (HDDs) is creating a shift in the storage controllers market.
