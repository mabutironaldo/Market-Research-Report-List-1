<p><ul><li>SEM</li><li>TEM</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Laboratory Electron Microscope market includes two main types, namely Scanning Electron Microscope (SEM) and Transmission Electron Microscope (TEM). SEM uses a focused beam of electrons to create a detailed 3D image of the specimen's surface, enabling high-resolution imaging and analysis. On the other hand, TEM transmits electrons through the specimen to create a high-resolution image on a fluorescent screen, providing information about the internal structure of the specimen. These types of electron microscopes are commonly used in laboratories for scientific research, material analysis, and medical diagnostics.</p></p>
