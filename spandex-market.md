<p><p>Spandex is a stretchable synthetic fiber widely used in various industries. It is produced through different market types, such as Solution Dry Spinning and Solution Wet Spinning. In Solution Dry Spinning, the spandex polymer is dissolved in a solvent and then extruded into air, resulting in soft and elastic fibers. On the other hand, Solution Wet Spinning involves dissolving the polymer in a solvent and then extruding it into a coagulation bath, creating strong and durable fibers. Besides these two main market types, there are other specialized methods utilized to produce specific types of spandex fibers for various applications.</p></p>
