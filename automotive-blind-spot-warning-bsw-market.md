<p><p>The Automotive Blind Spot Warning (BSW) market includes two main types: Active Blind Spot Monitoring and Passive Blind Spot Monitoring. Active Blind Spot Monitoring uses radar or sensors to detect vehicles in the driver's blind spot and provides alerts, such as visual or audible warnings, to the driver. It also has the capability to intervene by applying brakes or adjusting steering. On the other hand, Passive Blind Spot Monitoring relies on side-view mirrors or cameras to indicate the presence of vehicles in the blind spot, without actively intervening in the driving process.</p></p>
