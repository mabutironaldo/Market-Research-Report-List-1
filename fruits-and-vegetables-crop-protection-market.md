<p><p>The Fruits and Vegetables Crop Protection market can be categorized into three types: Synthetic Pesticides, Biopesticides, and Other market segments. Synthetic Pesticides refer to chemical-based products used to control pests and diseases, offering high effectiveness but raising concerns regarding environmental impact. Biopesticides, on the other hand, are derived from natural sources such as plants, bacteria, or animals, providing a more eco-friendly approach with minimal harm to non-targeted organisms. Lastly, the Other market segment consists of alternative methods like physical barriers, crop rotation, or genetic engineering, which also aim to protect the crops effectively but without the use of pesticides or biopesticides.</p></p>
