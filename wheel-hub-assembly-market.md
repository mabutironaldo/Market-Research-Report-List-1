<p><p>The wheel hub assembly market consists of different types of bearings that are used in vehicles. The different types include Gen. 1 Bearing, Gen. 2 Bearing, Gen. 3 Bearing, and Other Bearing. Gen. 1 Bearing refers to the first generation of wheel hub assemblies, which typically have an inner and outer ball bearing design. Gen. 2 Bearing includes a double row angular contact ball bearing and an integrated sensor for anti-lock braking system. Gen. 3 Bearing comprises a double row ball bearing with flange and integrated sensor. Other Bearing types refer to alternative bearing designs used in wheel hub assemblies.</p></p>
