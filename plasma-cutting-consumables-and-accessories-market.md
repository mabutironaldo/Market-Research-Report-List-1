<p><p>Plasma cutting consumables and accessories refer to various components used in the plasma cutting process for efficient and effective performance. Electrodes are responsible for generating the plasma arc, while nozzles and retaining caps control the flow and shape of the plasma stream. Shield caps protect the electrode and nozzle from damage, while swirl rings enhance gas flow and cut quality. Water tubes provide cooling for the system, and other accessories encompass a range of additional components that aid in the plasma cutting process, such as torch bodies and o-rings.</p></p>
