<p><p>Earthquake protection systems refer to various technologies used to mitigate the destructive impact of earthquakes on structures. Natural rubber bearings, lead rubber bearings, high damping rubber, and other systems are prevalent in the market. Natural rubber bearings consist of steel plates sandwiching layers of natural rubber, allowing for movement during an earthquake. Lead rubber bearings use a lead core surrounded by layers of rubber, providing high energy dissipation. High damping rubber systems absorb seismic energy through specialized rubber compounds. Other earthquake protection systems include friction pendulum bearings, isolation systems, and base isolation systems.</p></p>
