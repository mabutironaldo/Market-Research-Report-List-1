<p><p>The cosmetic dyes market consists of two main types: natural dyes and synthetic dyes. They offer a wide range of colors but can be less vibrant and have limited stability. Synthetic dyes, on the other hand, are chemically synthesized and offer greater color options, intensity, and stability.
