<p><ul><li>Type I Semi Flexible Cable</li><li>Type II Semi Flexible Cable</li></ul></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>There are two types of semi flexible cables in the market. Type I semi flexible cable refers to cables that have a solid outer conductor made of copper or aluminum, giving them high flexibility and a wide range of applications in industries like telecommunications and aerospace. On the other hand, Type II semi flexible cable features a corrugated outer conductor, offering improved electrical performance and durability, making them suitable for high-frequency applications such as radar systems and satellite communications. Both types cater to different needs and are widely used in various industries.</p></p>
