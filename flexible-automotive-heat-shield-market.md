<p><p>Flexible automotive heat shields are utilized to protect various components of a vehicle from excessive heat generated by the engine or exhaust system. Turbo heat shields shield the turbocharger from excessive heat, improving its performance and lifespan. Underbody heat shields protect the undercarriage components, including the fuel tank and suspension, from the heat emitted by the exhaust system. Header heat shields safeguard the headers or exhaust manifolds from extreme temperatures. Exhaust pipe heat shields provide insulation for the exhaust pipes, preventing heat transfer to surrounding areas and reducing the risk of damage or fire.</p></p>
