<p><p>The Vehicle Catalytic Converter market consists of several types, including two-way converters, three-way converters, and other market variants. Two-way converters primarily convert harmful carbon monoxide and unburned hydrocarbons into carbon dioxide and water vapor. Three-way converters also target nitrogen oxides, converting them into nitrogen and oxygen. Both two-way and three-way converters are widely used in vehicles to control emissions. Additionally, the market offers other converter types with unique features and benefits to cater to specific emission control requirements, providing a comprehensive range of options for vehicle manufacturers and consumers.</p></p>
