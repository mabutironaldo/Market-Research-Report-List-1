Due to these desirable characteristics, iron-based nanocrystalline soft magnetic materials find extensive applications in various industries, including power electronics, renewable energy, automotive, and industrial automation.</p><p>The market analysis of the iron-based nanocrystalline soft magnetic material indicates a positive growth outlook.
