<p><ul><li>Radial Tire</li><li>Bias Tire</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Dozer tire market is divided into two main types: radial tire market and bias tire market. Radial tires are constructed with layers of fabric that run radially from bead to bead, providing better stability, fuel efficiency, and longer tread life. Bias tires, on the other hand, are built with angled ply layers that crisscross over each other, giving them better sidewall flexibility and load-carrying capacity. Both types have their own advantages and are chosen based on specific needs, such as terrain conditions, load capacity, and cost considerations in the dozer industry.</p></p>
