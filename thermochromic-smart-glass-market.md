<p><p>Thermochromic smart glass refers to a type of glass that can change its transparency or color when exposed to heat or light. The market for thermochromic smart glass can be categorized into three types: low E-glasses, active glasses, and passive glasses. Low E-glasses have a special coating that improves energy efficiency by reducing heat transfer. Active glasses utilize an electric current or voltage to control the tint or transparency of the glass. On the other hand, passive glasses change their tint or transparency based on the temperature they are exposed to, without requiring any external power source.</p></p>
