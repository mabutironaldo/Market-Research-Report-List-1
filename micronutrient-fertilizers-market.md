<p><p>Micronutrient fertilizers are classified into several types based on their nutrient composition. Fe (iron) fertilizers are used to correct iron deficiency in plants, which is crucial for chlorophyll formation and photosynthesis. Mn (manganese) fertilizers are used to enhance plant growth, enzyme activity, and nitrogen metabolism. Zn (zinc) fertilizers are essential for carbohydrate metabolism and plant growth regulation. Cu (copper) fertilizers are utilized to improve enzyme activity and plant respiration. Combi fertilizers combine multiple micronutrients to provide a balanced nutrient supply to plants, ensuring their healthy growth.</p></p>
