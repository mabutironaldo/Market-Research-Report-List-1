<p><ul><li>Pneumatic</li><li>Solid</li><li>Polyurethane</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Field sprayers tires are available in different types in the market. Pneumatic tires are filled with air and provide a cushioned ride, ensuring better maneuverability and reducing soil compaction. Solid tires are made of solid rubber, eliminating the risk of punctures, but they have a stiffer ride and may cause more soil compaction. Polyurethane tires offer a balance between pneumatic and solid tires, providing cushioning effects while being puncture-proof. This variety of options allows farmers and field sprayer users to choose the most suitable tire type based on their specific needs and preferences.</p></p>
