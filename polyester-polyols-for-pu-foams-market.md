<p><p>Polyester polyols for PU foams are types of materials used in the production of polyurethane foams. They are produced using different methods such as the vacuum melting method, carrier gas melting method, and azeotropic vapor method. In the vacuum melting method, the polyester polyols are melted under vacuum conditions to remove impurities. The carrier gas melting method involves using a carrier gas to melt the polyester polyols. The azeotropic vapor method utilizes azeotropic distillation to obtain the desired polyester polyols for PU foam production.</p></p>
