<p><p>Construction lubricants refer to various types of lubricating oils and greases used in the construction industry to minimize friction and enhance the performance and lifespan of machinery and equipment. Hydraulic fluids are used in hydraulic systems, while engine oils are employed in construction equipment engines. Gear oils ensure efficient gearing operation, whereas automatic transmission fluids (ATF) are used in automatic transmissions. Compressor oils are utilized in air compressors, and greases offer lubrication to various construction components. These lubricants are crucial for smooth operation and optimal efficiency in construction machinery and equipment.</p></p>
