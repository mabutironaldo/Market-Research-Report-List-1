Additionally, the growing pharmaceutical industry, coupled with the increasing demand for pharmaceutical intermediates, is further driving the demand for diphenylmethanol.</p><p>Moreover, the market is also witnessing a rising demand for dyes and pigments, where diphenylmethanol is used as an intermediate.
