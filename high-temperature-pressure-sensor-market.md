<p><p>The high temperature pressure sensor market consists of two main types: high temperature melt pressure sensors and ultra-high temperature pressure sensors. High temperature melt pressure sensors are designed to measure the pressure in high-temperature manufacturing processes, such as plastic extrusion. They can withstand temperatures up to 400 degrees Celsius. On the other hand, ultra-high temperature pressure sensors are capable of measuring pressure in extreme conditions, with temperatures reaching up to 1000 degrees Celsius. These sensors are commonly used in industries like aerospace and automotive, where high temperatures are encountered.</p></p>
