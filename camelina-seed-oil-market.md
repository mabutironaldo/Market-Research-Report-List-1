<p><p>Camelina seed oil comes in two market types: unrefined camellia oil and refined camellia oil. Unrefined camellia oil, also known as cold-pressed or virgin oil, is extracted from the seeds using a mechanical pressing method without any additional processing. It retains its natural characteristics, including its distinct flavor and aroma. On the other hand, refined camellia oil undergoes additional processing steps, such as filtering and refining, to remove impurities and improve its shelf life. This process also results in a refined oil with a milder taste and higher smoke point.</p></p>
