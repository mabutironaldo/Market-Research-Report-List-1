<p><p>The Reforming Catalyst market can be divided into two different types: Reforming CRRCatalysts and Reforming SRRCatalysts. Reforming CRRCatalysts are used in the catalytic reforming process to convert low-octane naphtha into high-octane gasoline. They have a higher activity level, ensuring efficient conversion and production. On the other hand, Reforming SRRCatalysts are employed in the semi-regenerative reforming process, emphasizing stability and enhanced selectivity. Both catalyst types play crucial roles in the petroleum refining industry, catering to different reforming processes while aiming for optimal performance and product quality.</p></p>
