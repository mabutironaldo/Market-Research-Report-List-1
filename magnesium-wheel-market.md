<p><p>The magnesium wheel market can be classified into three types: cast magnesium wheel, forged magnesium wheel, and others. Cast magnesium wheels are created by pouring molten magnesium alloy into a mold, resulting in a lightweight wheel with good impact resistance but limited strength. Forged magnesium wheels are formed by subjecting magnesium alloy to high pressure and heat, creating a wheel with enhanced strength and durability. The "others" segment includes various types of magnesium wheels, such as hybrid or composite wheels, which combine magnesium with other materials to maximize performance.</p></p>
