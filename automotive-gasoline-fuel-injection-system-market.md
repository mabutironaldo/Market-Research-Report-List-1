<p><p>The automotive gasoline fuel injection system market comprises several types. Single-point or throttle body injection systems deliver fuel through a single injector located at the throttle body. Port or multipoint fuel injection systems have separate injectors for each cylinder, ensuring precise fuel distribution. Sequential fuel injection systems supply fuel to the cylinders in a specific order, offering improved fuel efficiency. Direct injection systems accurately inject fuel directly into the combustion chamber, enhancing power and efficiency. Lastly, there are other emerging fuel injection technologies being developed in the market.</p></p>
