<p><p>The portable thermal dilatometers market consists of various types of instruments used for measuring dimensional changes in materials due to temperature variations. Capacitance thermal dilatometers employ the principle of capacitance change to measure expansion or contraction of materials. Connecting rod thermal dilatometers are specifically designed to measure the linear thermal expansion of connecting rods. Optical thermal dilatometers use optical interferometry to measure changes in length or volume of materials. These different types of dilatometers are used in industries such as aerospace, automotive, and materials science for precise thermal analysis.</p></p>
