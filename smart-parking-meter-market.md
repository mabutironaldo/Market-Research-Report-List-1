<p><p>The smart parking meter market is divided into two types: single space and multi space. Single space parking meters are primarily used in areas where parking spaces are limited, such as busy city streets. These meters are installed at individual parking spaces and allow users to pay for their parking time using various methods like coins, cards, or mobile apps. On the other hand, multi space parking meters are commonly found in parking lots or garages. These meters serve multiple parking spaces and offer more advanced features like license plate recognition and centralized payment systems.</p></p>
