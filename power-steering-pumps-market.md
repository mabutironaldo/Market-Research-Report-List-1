<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Power Steering Pumps Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Blade Type Steering Pump</li><li>Gear Type Steering Pump</li><li>Plunger Type Steering Pump</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Power steering pumps are categorized into three main types - blade type steering pumps, gear type steering pumps, and plunger type steering pumps. Blade type pumps use radial blades to create pressure and are generally used in low-pressure applications. Gear type pumps consist of intermeshing gears that create pressure and are commonly used in high-pressure applications. Plunger type pumps use reciprocating plungers to generate pressure and are primarily used in heavy-duty applications. These different types of power steering pumps offer distinct advantages based on the specific requirements of the steering system.</p></p>
