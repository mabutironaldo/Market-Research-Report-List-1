<p><p>The OLED Universal Material market includes several types of materials that are essential for the functioning of OLED displays. This includes Electron Transport Layer (ETL), Electron Injection Layer (EIL), Hole Injection Layer (HIL), Hole Transport Layer (HTL), and other materials. ETL facilitates the movement of electrons, EIL aids in the injection of electrons into the organic layer, HIL helps in injecting holes, and HTL assists in the movement of holes. These materials collectively ensure the proper functioning of OLED displays by enabling the efficient flow of charges and light emission.</p></p>
