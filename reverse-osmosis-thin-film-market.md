<p><p>Reverse osmosis thin film is a technology used for water purification. Cellulose thin film is a type of reverse osmosis membrane made from cellulose acetate, which effectively removes contaminants but is vulnerable to deterioration over time. On the other hand, composite thin film is another type of reverse osmosis membrane constructed using a blend of different materials such as polymer and ceramic. It offers superior durability and resistance to fouling, making it highly efficient for long-term water filtration applications. Both types contribute to the thriving market of reverse osmosis thin film technologies.</p></p>
