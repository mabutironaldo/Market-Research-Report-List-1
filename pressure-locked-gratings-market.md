<p><p>Pressure locked gratings are a type of structural grating made from pressed bearing bars and crossbars that interlock together under intense hydraulic pressure. Pressure locked gratings are commonly used as flooring, stair treads, walkways, and platforms due to their high strength, anti-slip characteristics, and ability to withstand heavy loads.</p><p>The current outlook of the pressure locked gratings market is positive and is expected to experience significant growth in the coming years.
