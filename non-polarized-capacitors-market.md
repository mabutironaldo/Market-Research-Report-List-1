<p><p>Non-polarized capacitors, also known as non-electrolytic capacitors, are passive electrical components that store energy in an electric field. Non-polarized capacitors are commonly used in AC circuits and audio applications where the polarity of the voltage changes frequently.</p><p>The non-polarized capacitors market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 8.7% during the forecast period. These capacitors are used in various automotive electronics, including engine control units, power steering, and infotainment systems.
