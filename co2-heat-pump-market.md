<p><p>The CO2 heat pump market consists of different types such as air to water monobloc, air to water cylinder, air to water split, and exhaust air. Air to water monobloc systems use CO2 as a refrigerant to transfer heat from the air to water for heating applications. Air to water cylinder systems also use CO2 refrigerant but with an additional water storage cylinder. Air to water split systems consist of an outdoor unit to extract heat and an indoor unit to distribute hot water or heat. Exhaust air systems recover heat from exhaust air for various heating purposes.</p></p>
