<p><p>IPM Pheromones refer to insect pheromones used in Integrated Pest Management strategies. Sex pheromones are used to attract and capture male insects, disrupting their reproductive cycle. Aggregation pheromones are utilized to lure insects towards traps, instigating mass capture. These two pheromone types help reduce pest populations. Other types of pheromones, such as alarm or trail pheromones, are also used for insect monitoring or control. Overall, IPM Pheromones offer a chemical-based approach to pest management by exploiting the natural communication signals of insects and disrupting their behaviors.</p></p>
