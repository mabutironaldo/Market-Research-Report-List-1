<p><ul><li>Boneless Wiper Blade</li><li>Bone Wiper Blade</li><li>Hybrid Wiper Blade</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The market for vehicle wiper blades can be divided into three primary types: boneless wiper blades, bone wiper blades, and hybrid wiper blades. Boneless wiper blades are made from a single molded piece of rubber, ensuring a smooth and streak-free wiping action. Bone wiper blades feature a frame made of metal or plastic that holds a rubber blade, providing durability and flexibility. Hybrid wiper blades combine the advantages of both boneless and bone wiper blades, often featuring a sleek and aerodynamic design. These different types cater to varying customer preferences and offer unique features for optimal windshield cleaning.</p></p>
