<p><p>Textile dyes are used to add color to fabrics and textiles. The market for these dyes can be categorized into various types. Disperse dyes are mainly used for polyester and acetate fibers. Reactive dyes form a covalent bond with the fabric, resulting in excellent colorfastness. Sulfur dyes are commonly used for cellulosic fibers, offering good washing resistance. Vat dyes are used for natural fibers like cotton and have high colorfastness. Acid dyes are used for protein fibers like wool and silk. Lastly, there are other types of dyes available for specific fabric types or special applications.</p></p>
