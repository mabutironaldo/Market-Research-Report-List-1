<p><p>The modular motorcycle helmet market consists of three main types of materials: ABS, fiberglass reinforced plastic, and carbon fiber. ABS helmets are made from a blend of acrylonitrile, butadiene, and styrene, offering good impact resistance and affordability. Fiberglass reinforced plastic helmets have a layer of glass fiber embedded in a plastic resin, providing a balance of strength and weight. Carbon fiber helmets are constructed from carbon fibers incorporated in a resin matrix, ensuring extremely lightweight and high strength properties. These materials cater to different consumer preferences in terms of budget, durability, and weight.</p></p>
