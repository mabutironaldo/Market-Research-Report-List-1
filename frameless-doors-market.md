<p><p>Frameless doors, also known as glass doors, are doors that have minimal or no visible frames around the edges. Frameless doors can be hinged, sliding, or folding, and are commonly used in commercial buildings, hotels, residential properties, and shower enclosures.</p><p>The frameless doors market has seen significant growth in recent years and is expected to continue expanding at a compound annual growth rate (CAGR) of 8.8% during the forecast period. This growth can be attributed to several factors. Firstly, the rising demand for aesthetically appealing and modern interior and exterior solutions has increased the adoption of frameless doors. Additionally, the increasing trend of open floor plans and large windows in residential and commercial buildings has boosted the demand for frameless doors as they allow uninterrupted views and natural light to flow into spaces.</p><p>Moreover, the high emphasis on energy efficiency and sustainability in building constructions has further fueled the market growth. Frameless doors offer excellent thermal insulation properties, reducing energy consumption and enhancing the overall eco-friendliness of a building.</p><p>In terms of the market trends, manufacturers are focusing on product innovations and technological advancements to cater to the evolving consumer demands. Integration of smart technologies such as touchless sensors and remote control systems is gaining traction in the market. Furthermore, the customization of frameless doors to match specific design and size requirements is becoming increasingly popular among consumers.</p><p>Overall, the frameless doors market is poised for substantial growth due to the increasing construction of commercial and residential buildings and the rising focus on energy efficiency and aesthetics. The market is likely to witness advancements in technology and new product launches to cater to the growing consumer expectations.</p></p>
