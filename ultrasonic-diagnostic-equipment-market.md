<p><p>Ultrasonic diagnostic equipment refers to medical devices used for imaging internal body structures using ultrasound waves. There are two types of ultrasound equipment in the market: Continuous Wave (CW) and Pulsed Wave (PW). Continuous wave ultrasound equipment emits a continuous ultrasound signal and is mainly used for Doppler ultrasound applications, such as measuring blood flow. Pulsed wave ultrasound equipment emits short bursts of ultrasound waves and is commonly used for imaging organs and tissues, allowing for better resolution and accuracy.</p></p>
