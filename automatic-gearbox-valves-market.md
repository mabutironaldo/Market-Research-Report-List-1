Secondly, Shift Control Valves manage the process of shifting gears smoothly and accurately. Thirdly, Timing Valves ensure the precise timing of gear changes. Lastly, Pressure Modulating Valves regulate the pressure exerted by the hydraulic system in the gearbox. These valves play crucial roles in ensuring the efficient operation and performance of automatic gearboxes in vehicles.</p></p>
