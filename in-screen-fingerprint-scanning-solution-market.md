<p><p>The in-screen fingerprint scanning solution market comprises two main types: optical and ultrasonic. Optical scanners utilize light to create a 2D image of the fingerprint, which is then analyzed for identification. On the other hand, ultrasonic scanners employ sound waves to create a 3D model of the fingerprint, providing enhanced security. While optical scanners are more affordable and widely adopted, ultrasonic scanners offer higher accuracy and reliability. Both solutions cater to the growing demand for convenient and secure biometric authentication on smartphones and other electronic devices.</p></p>
