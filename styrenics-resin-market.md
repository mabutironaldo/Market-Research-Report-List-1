<p><strong>The Styrenics Resin Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>

<p><p>The styrenics resin market consists of several types of resins, including GPPS, HIPS, SAN, and ABS. GPPS (General Purpose Polystyrene) is a versatile, transparent resin used in a wide range of applications such as packaging materials and consumer goods. HIPS (High Impact Polystyrene) offers enhanced impact resistance and is commonly used in the production of appliances and toys. SAN (Styrene Acrylonitrile) provides improved heat and chemical resistance, making it suitable for electronic and automotive components. ABS (Acrylonitrile Butadiene Styrene) is a tough and durable resin ideal for products like automotive parts, pipes, and electronic enclosures.</p></p>
