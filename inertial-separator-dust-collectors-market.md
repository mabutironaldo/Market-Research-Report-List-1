<p><p>Inertial Separator Dust Collectors market includes two main types - Colliding Type and Rotary Type. Colliding Type dust collectors operate by using a sudden change in direction to force dust particles to collide with the collection surface, where they are then collected. On the other hand, Rotary Type dust collectors utilize centrifugal force to separate the dust particles from the airflow, collecting them in a drum or hopper. Both types of dust collectors are utilized in various industries to efficiently capture and remove airborne dust and improve air quality.</p></p>
