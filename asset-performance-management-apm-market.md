<p><p>Asset Performance Management (APM) encompasses various market types, including Asset Integrity Management and Asset Reliability Management. Asset Integrity Management focuses on ensuring the structural soundness and operational safety of assets throughout their lifecycle. This involves monitoring and managing the asset's condition, conducting inspections and tests, and implementing maintenance strategies to prevent failures and extend its lifespan. On the other hand, Asset Reliability Management concentrates on optimizing asset performance by minimizing downtime, improving maintenance practices, and enhancing equipment reliability to maximize efficiency and profitability. Both market types aim to ensure optimal asset performance and minimize operational risks.</p></p>
