<p><p>Pour Point Depressant (PPD) is an additive used in petroleum products to lower their pour point temperature, which is the lowest temperature at which the fuel or oil can flow. The market is driven by the increasing demand for petroleum products, especially in regions with colder climates where low-temperature flowability is crucial. The rising demand for automobiles and transportation fuels also contributes to the growth of the market. Additionally, the growth of the oil and gas industry, especially in emerging economies, further boosts the demand for PPDs.</p><p>Furthermore, stricter environmental regulations regarding emissions and fuel efficiency are driving the market growth.
