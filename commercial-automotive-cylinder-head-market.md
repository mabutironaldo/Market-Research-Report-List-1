They are commonly used in heavy-duty applications due to their durability.</p><p>Allory Cast Iron Type cylinder heads are manufactured using a blend of gray cast iron and other alloys, enhancing their strength and resistance to heat. These heads are suitable for both light and heavy-duty vehicles.</p><p>Aluminum Type cylinder heads are crafted from aluminum, offering lightweight properties and excellent heat dissipation. They are preferred in high-performance and sports vehicles as they improve fuel efficiency and allow for faster engine response.</p></p>
