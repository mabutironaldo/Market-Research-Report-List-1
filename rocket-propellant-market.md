<p><p>The rocket propellant market is categorized into four types based on their physical state and composition. Solid rocket propellant is a mixture of fuel and oxidizer in a solid form. Liquid rocket propellant consists of liquid fuel and oxidizer stored separately and mixed together during combustion. Gaseous rocket propellant uses compressed gases, such as hydrogen or oxygen. Hybrid rocket propellant is a combination of solid and liquid propellants, with one being a solid fuel and the other a liquid oxidizer. These propellants play a crucial role in powering different types of rockets for space exploration and military applications.</p></p>
