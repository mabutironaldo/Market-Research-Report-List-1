<p><p>The automotive traction control system market encompasses three main types: mechanical systems, hydraulic systems, and electric systems. Mechanical systems utilize mechanical connections to monitor and control wheel spin, ensuring optimal traction. Hydraulic systems employ hydraulic pressure to limit wheel spin and regulate power distribution. Electric systems utilize electronic sensors and control units to monitor wheel slippage and adjust power accordingly. These different types of systems provide vehicles with enhanced traction and stability, improving overall performance and safety, particularly in challenging road conditions.</p></p>
