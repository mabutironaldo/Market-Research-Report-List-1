<p><p>Endoscopy capsules are small, swallowable devices equipped with tiny cameras that aid in the diagnosis and treatment of gastrointestinal (GI) disorders. These capsules capture images and transmit them wirelessly to a receiver, enabling doctors to visualize the GI tract and diagnose conditions such as Crohn's disease, gastrointestinal bleeding, and colon polyps. Endoscopy capsules offer advantages like non-invasiveness, patient comfort, and ease of use, making them a preferred alternative to traditional endoscopic procedures.</p><p>The global endoscopy capsules market is experiencing significant growth, with a CAGR of 12.1% expected during the forecast period. Several factors drive this growth. Firstly, the rising prevalence of GI disorders such as colorectal cancer and gastrointestinal bleeding is increasing the demand for accurate and early diagnostic techniques, promoting market growth. Additionally, the preference for non-invasive procedures, reduced hospital stays, and advancements in imaging technology drive the adoption of endoscopy capsules.</p><p>Technological advancements in the endoscopy capsule field are also driving market growth.
