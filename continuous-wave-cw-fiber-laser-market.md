<p><p>Continuous Wave (CW) Fiber Laser Market Types are categorized based on the kind of fiber used in the lasers. Nonlinear Optical Fiber Lasers utilize fibers with nonlinear properties, allowing for efficient frequency conversion or mode locking. Plastic Fiber Lasers employ polymer-based materials for laser generation. Other market types include glass fiber lasers and metal fiber lasers.
