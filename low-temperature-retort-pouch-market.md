<p><p>The low temperature retort pouch market consists of two main types: stand-up pouches and flat pouches. Stand-up pouches are flexible packaging bags that have a gusset at the bottom, allowing them to stand upright on store shelves. These pouches offer convenience and easy display, making them popular for various food products. On the other hand, flat pouches are also flexible packaging bags but do not have a gusset, remaining flat in shape. These pouches are suitable for products that do not require standing display or have limited space.</p></p>
