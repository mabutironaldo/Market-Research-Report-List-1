<p><p>The tungsten sheets market can be classified into two types: alloy and pure metal. Alloy tungsten sheets are made by combining tungsten with other metals, resulting in improved properties such as increased hardness and resistance to corrosion. These sheets are commonly used in the aerospace and automotive industries. On the other hand, pure metal tungsten sheets are made solely from tungsten and offer high density and heat resistance. They are extensively used in electrical applications and in the production of heating elements and vacuum furnace parts.</p></p>
