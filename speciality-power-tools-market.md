<p><p>Specialty power tools are categorized into two types based on their mode of operation - wireless and wired. Wireless power tools, also known as cordless power tools, are powered by rechargeable batteries and do not require a direct electrical connection. They offer greater mobility and flexibility, making them suitable for working in remote locations or areas with limited power sources. On the other hand, wired power tools need to be connected to an electrical outlet for operation. They provide a continuous power supply, ensuring consistent performance but restricting movement to the length of the power cord.</p></p>
