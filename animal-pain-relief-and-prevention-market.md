<p><p>Animal pain relief and prevention market can be categorized into two types: external use and internal use. </p><p>External use pain relief products include topical solutions, creams, ointments, and sprays that are applied on the animal's skin or coat. These products are effective in providing immediate relief from pain and inflammation caused by injuries, wounds, or arthritis.</p><p>Internal use pain relief products, on the other hand, include oral medications, injectables, and supplements that are ingested or administered directly to the animal's body. These products target internal sources of pain, such as inflammation, nerve damage, or post-surgical pain, providing long-term relief and prevention. Both types aim to improve animal well-being and enhance their overall quality of life.</p></p>
