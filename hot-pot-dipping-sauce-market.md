<p><p>Hot pot dipping sauces can be classified into two main types: solid type and liquid type. Solid type sauces are usually in the form of paste or powder, which need to be mixed with water or oil to create a dip. They often have a concentrated flavor and are known for their stronger taste and thicker texture. On the other hand, liquid type sauces are ready-to-use and do not require any additional preparation. They are usually in the form of a sauce or dressing and offer a more convenient option for hot pot enthusiasts.</p></p>
