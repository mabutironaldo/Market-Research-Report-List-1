<p><p>The aerospace joints market consists of various types of joints that play a crucial role in the aerospace industry. Gimbal joints allow rotational movement around multiple axes, enabling flexibility in aircraft control systems. Ball joints facilitate smooth movement and pivot with minimal friction, enhancing maneuverability in critical areas. Swivel joints provide rotational movement in one plane, improving flexibility and durability in aircraft hydraulic and fuel systems.
