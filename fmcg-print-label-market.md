<p><p>The FMCG print label market offers various types of labels for different purposes. Wet glued labels are traditional labels that are applied using a wet adhesive. Pressure sensitive/self adhesive labels have a sticky backing and can be easily applied by simply pressing them onto a surface. Liner-less labels do not have any backing and are commonly used in the food industry. Multi-part barcode labels have different sections for tracking information. In-mold labels are applied during the manufacturing process. Shrink sleeve labels are made of plastic and shrink to fit tightly around a product's packaging.</p></p>
