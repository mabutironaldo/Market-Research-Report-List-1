<p><p>The Sun Control Window Film market consists of three main types: clear (non-reflective), dyed (non-reflective), and vacuum coated (reflective). Clear films are designed to minimize the amount of heat and UV rays entering a space without affecting visibility. Dyed films use a layer of dye to reduce heat and glare, offering privacy while still allowing natural light to pass through. Vacuum coated films have a thin metallic layer that reflects solar energy, providing superior heat reduction and privacy, but may affect visibility. These films are used to improve energy efficiency and enhance comfort in buildings.</p></p>
