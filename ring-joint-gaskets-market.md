<p><p>The Ring Joint Gaskets market includes three main types: Style R, Style RX, and Style BX. Style R gaskets are suitable for flanges with narrow surface finish or low bolt loads. Style RX gaskets are designed for high-pressure applications with higher bolt loads. Style BX gaskets are used in very high-pressure applications and have a modified metal ring to enhance sealing performance. These gaskets are widely used in industries such as oil and gas, petrochemicals, and refining for their ability to withstand high temperatures and pressures while ensuring a reliable seal.</p></p>
