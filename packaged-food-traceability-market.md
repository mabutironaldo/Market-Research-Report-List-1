Barcodes are the most widely used technology, allowing for data capture and identification. RFID (Radio Frequency Identification) uses wireless technology to automatically track and identify products. GPS (Global Positioning System) enables tracking the location of products throughout the supply chain. Infrared technology monitors temperature during transportation and storage. Biometrics involves the use of unique physical attributes like fingerprints or facial recognition to ensure the authenticity and integrity of the food products. These technologies collectively enhance transparency and safety in the packaged food industry.</p></p>
