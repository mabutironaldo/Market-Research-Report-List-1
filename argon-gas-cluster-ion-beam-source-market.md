<p><p>Argon Gas Cluster Ion Beam Source is used in various industries for surface modification and analysis purposes. The market can be categorized into two types: Sputter Beams and Analytical Beams. Sputter Beams are used for thin film deposition and etching processes, whereby the Argon ions dislodge atoms from the target material's surface. Analytical Beams, on the other hand, are utilized for surface analysis techniques such as secondary ion mass spectrometry (SIMS) and time-of-flight mass spectrometry (TOF-SIMS) to analyze the elemental and molecular composition of a material's surface.</p></p>
