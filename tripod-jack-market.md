<p><p>In the market for tripod jacks, two types can be distinguished: single stage and multi-stage. A single-stage tripod jack has a fixed height and is mostly used for standard applications with a consistent lifting requirement. On the other hand, multi-stage tripod jacks offer adjustable heights, allowing users to adapt to various lifting needs in different scenarios. These jacks are commonly preferred in industries that require flexibility and versatility in lifting operations.
