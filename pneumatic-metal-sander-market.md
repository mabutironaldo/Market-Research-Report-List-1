<p><p>A pneumatic metal sander is a handheld power tool that is used for smoothing and polishing metal surfaces. One of the key drivers of this growth is the increasing demand for efficient and effective metalworking tools in various industries. Pneumatic metal sanders offer several advantages over their electric counterparts, including higher power-to-weight ratios, longer lifespans, and better control over speed and power. These factors contribute to their popularity and widespread use in the market.</p><p>Another trend observed in the pneumatic metal sander market is the introduction of innovative features and technologies.
