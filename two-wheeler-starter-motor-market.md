<p><p>The two-wheeler starter motor market comprises three types: electric, pneumatic, and hydraulic starter motors. Electric starter motors use electrical energy to crank the engine and are commonly used in modern motorcycles for their reliability and ease of use. Pneumatic starter motors, on the other hand, harness compressed air to start the engine, making them suitable for heavy-duty applications. Hydraulic starter motors use hydraulic power to initiate the engine, providing a steady and consistent starting force. Each type offers unique benefits, catering to diverse preferences and requirements in the two-wheeler market.</p></p>
