<p><p>A digital compound machine, also known as a CNC (Computer Numerical Control) compound machine, is a type of machining equipment that uses computer-aided design and manufacturing techniques to automate and enhance the production process. It combines multiple machine tools into a single unit, such as milling, turning, grinding, drilling, and boring machines, to perform various operations on a workpiece. This integration leads to increased precision, accuracy, speed, and efficiency in manufacturing processes.</p><p>The current outlook of the digital compound machine market is promising and shows significant growth potential.
