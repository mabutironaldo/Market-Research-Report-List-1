<p><strong>The Under display Fingerprint Module Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Capacitive Fingerprint Module</li><li>Optical Fingerprint Module</li><li>Ultrasonic Fingerprint Module Module</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The under display fingerprint module market can be categorized into three types: capacitive, optical, and ultrasonic fingerprint modules. Capacitive modules work by detecting the electrical properties of a finger's ridges and valleys. Optical modules capture an image of the finger using light to identify the unique pattern. Ultrasonic modules use soundwaves to create a detailed 3D image of the finger's ridges, enabling accurate identification. Each type has its own advantages and limitations, ranging from accuracy to speed, and is utilized in various devices that offer under-display fingerprint recognition technology.</p></p>
