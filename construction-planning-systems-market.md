<p><p>Construction planning systems are software tools used by construction companies to manage and streamline their planning processes. They can be categorized into two main types: cloud-based and on-premises. Cloud-based systems are web-based platforms hosted on remote servers, allowing users to access them through the internet. They offer advantages such as flexibility, scalability, and remote access. On the other hand, on-premises systems are installed locally on the company's own servers, providing complete control and customization but requiring maintenance and updates to be performed in-house.</p></p>
