<p><ul><li>Integrated</li><li>Portable</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The bus tachograph market is categorized into two types: integrated and portable. Integrated tachographs are built into the dashboard of the bus and are permanently installed. They offer a seamless and automatic recording of driver and vehicle data, ensuring compliance with regulations. On the other hand, portable tachographs are a separate device that can be easily removed and used in various vehicles. They provide flexibility for bus operators with multiple vehicles, allowing the same device to be utilized across different buses for data recording purposes.</p></p>
