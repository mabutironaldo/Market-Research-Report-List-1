<p><p>A precision planetary speed reducer, also known as a precision gearbox, is a mechanical device used to reduce the rotational speed of an input shaft while increasing the torque output. These gears rotate around the central axis, rotating the output shaft at a reduced speed compared to the input shaft.</p><p>The precision planetary speed reducer market is experiencing significant growth due to various factors.
