<p><p>The PVC/PVG solid woven conveyor belt market consists of two main types: PVC conveyor belt and PVG conveyor belt. It is widely used in industries such as food processing and packaging, mining, and agriculture. PVG conveyor belt, on the other hand, is made of a combination of materials, including polyvinyl chloride and rubber. It provides high flame resistance and is commonly used in underground mining operations for transporting coal and other materials.</p></p>
