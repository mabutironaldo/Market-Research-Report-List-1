<p><p>Cracking Catalysts for the Propylene market are divided into several types. These include CHP (Chemical-Physical Hybrid), CRP (Chemical-Reactor-Promoted), CIP (Chemical-Intrinsic Promotion), MMC (Mixed Matrix Catalysts), and others. CHP catalysts are formed by combining both chemical and physical components, enhancing propylene selectivity. CRP catalysts use reactor-promoters to improve the propylene production process. CIP catalysts work by promoting specific chemical reactions. MMC catalysts comprise mixtures of different materials to optimize propylene production. Each of these catalyst types offers unique advantages and plays a crucial role in the propylene cracking process.</p></p>
