<p><p>The Hemorrhoid Treatment Instrument market consists of various types of devices used for treating hemorrhoids. Band ligators are used to tie off the hemorrhoid with a rubber band, cutting off its blood supply. Sclerotherapy injectors involve injecting a chemical solution into the hemorrhoid to shrink it. Infrared coagulators utilize infrared light to cut off blood supply to the hemorrhoid. Bipolar probes use electric currents to coagulate the hemorrhoid. Cryotherapy devices freeze the hemorrhoid, causing it to shrink. These devices offer different methods for treating hemorrhoids effectively.</p></p>
