<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The oil offloading systems market is segmented into two types: Conventional Buoy Mooring (CBM) and Single Point Mooring (SPM). CBM involves a buoy anchored to the seabed, which connects the oil tanker through multiple flexible hoses for offloading. SPM, on the other hand, implements a single buoy connected directly to the tanker through a flexible hose, simplifying the offloading process. These systems facilitate the transfer of oil from offshore platforms to tankers, ensuring efficient and safe transportation of oil products.</p></p>
