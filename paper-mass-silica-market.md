<p><ul><li>Precipitated Silica</li><li>Fumed Silica</li><li>Colloidal Silica</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The paper mass silica market includes various types of silica used in the paper industry. Precipitated silica is a synthetic form produced by chemical precipitation and is widely used in paper coatings and fillers. Fumed silica is a fine powder manufactured through the vapor-phase process and is utilized for enhancing ink absorption and improving paper quality. Colloidal silica is a stable suspension of nanoscale silica particles in water, commonly employed as a binder in papermaking. "Others" category comprises additional silica forms like silica gel, silica nanoparticles, and silica microspheres, among others, which have unique applications in the paper industry.</p></p>
