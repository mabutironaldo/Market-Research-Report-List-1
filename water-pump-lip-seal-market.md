<p><p>The water pump lip seal market consists of four types: reciprocating type, oscillating type, rotary type, and static type. Reciprocating type seals are used in water pumps with up and down motion, while oscillating type seals are designed for pumps with side-to-side motion. Rotary type seals are suitable for pumps with spinning motion, and static type seals are employed in stationary water pumps. These different types cater to various pump mechanisms and ensure effective sealing to prevent water leakage.</p></p>
