<p><p>The automotive tie rod assembly is a crucial component of the steering system in vehicles. It connects the steering knuckles to the center link or rack and pinion, allowing the wheels to be turned. The tie rod assembly consists of an inner and outer tie rod end, a steering linkage rod, and various connecting parts. These components work together to enable smooth and controlled steering.</p><p>Market Analysis:</p><p>The global automotive tie rod assembly market is anticipated to show substantial growth during the forecast period. Factors such as increasing vehicle production, rising consumer demand for performance and safety enhancements in vehicles, and the need for regular maintenance and replacement of worn-out tie rod assemblies contribute to market growth.
