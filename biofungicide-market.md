<p><p>The biofungicide market consists of four main types: agricultural antibiotics, bacterium fungicides, fungi fungicides, and virus fungicides. Agricultural antibiotics are substances derived from microorganisms that suppress fungal growth in crops. Bacterium fungicides contain live bacteria that can inhibit and control fungal pathogens. Fungi fungicides consist of microorganisms that are parasitic to plant pathogens and prevent their growth. Virus fungicides use viruses to target and control specific plant diseases. These biofungicides offer eco-friendly and sustainable solutions to manage plant diseases and enhance crop productivity.</p></p>
