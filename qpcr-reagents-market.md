<p><strong>The qPCR Reagents Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Dye-based Reagents</li><li>Probe & Primer-based Reagents</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The qPCR Reagents Market can be categorized into two types: Dye-based Reagents and Probe & Primer-based Reagents. Dye-based reagents use fluorescent dyes to mark DNA amplification during the qPCR process. These dyes emit fluorescence when bound to the amplified DNA, allowing for real-time monitoring of the process. On the other hand, Probe & Primer-based reagents utilize specific probes and primers to target and amplify DNA sequences of interest. These reagents offer higher specificity and accuracy in qPCR results, making them suitable for various applications.</p></p>
