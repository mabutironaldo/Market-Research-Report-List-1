<p><p>Coating agents for synthetic leather are used to enhance the appearance, texture, and durability of the material. The market for these agents can be categorized into four types. Polyurethane (PU) coating agents provide a soft, flexible, and breathable coating, making it suitable for various applications. Polyvinyl Chloride (PVC) coating agents offer excellent resistance to water, scratches, and chemicals, making it a preferred choice for high-performance synthetic leather. Silicone coating agents create a smooth and glossy finish while having good wear resistance properties. Lastly, the "Other" market includes various coating agents with unique characteristics, such as water-based coatings or eco-friendly options.</p></p>
