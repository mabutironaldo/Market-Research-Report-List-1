<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>Flatbed heat transfer printing machines market can be classified into three types: manual, semi-automatic, and fully automatic. </p><p>1. Manual machines require manual handling and control by operators for tasks like placing and removing the substrate, as well as adjusting temperature and pressure settings. They are suitable for small-scale operations with low production volumes requiring human intervention.</p><p>2. Semi-automatic machines offer certain automated features, such as an automated substrate feeding system or pressure adjustment. These machines reduce the operator's workload and can handle medium-scale production volumes.</p><p>3. Fully automatic machines are highly automated and require minimal human intervention. They come with automated features like substrate feeding, temperature and pressure control, and even image registration. These machines are suitable for large-scale production with high efficiency and productivity.</p></p>
