<p><p>The home nursing bed market consists of two types: manual nursing beds and electric nursing beds. Manual nursing beds are operated manually, without the need for any electrical power or assistance. They typically have adjustable height and backrest features to enhance comfort and accommodate various patient needs. On the other hand, electric nursing beds are powered by electricity and offer additional functions like electric adjustments of height, backrest, and leg positions. These electric beds are designed to provide better support, convenience, and ease of use for both patients and caregivers.</p></p>
