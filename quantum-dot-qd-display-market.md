<p><p>The Quantum Dot (QD) Display market comprises two main types: Quantum Dot Enhancement Film (QDEF) and Quantum Dot Light Emitting Diode (QLED). QDEF utilizes a thin film containing Quantum Dots that enhances the color gamut and brightness of LCD panels by converting blue light into other colors. On the other hand, QLED is a self-emissive display technology that directly emits light using Quantum Dots. It offers improved contrast, color accuracy, and energy efficiency compared to traditional OLED or LCD screens, making it a promising next-generation display technology.</p></p>
