<p><p>Air cooled chillers are cooling systems used to remove heat from a space or material. The market includes two main types: air cooled scroll chiller and air cooled screw chiller. Air cooled scroll chillers use scroll compressor technology and are more suitable for small to medium-sized applications. They are compact, energy efficient, and have fewer moving parts. On the other hand, air cooled screw chillers use screw compressor technology and are used in larger scale applications. They provide higher cooling capacities and are often used in industrial settings.</p></p>
