<p><p>CFD Trading Affiliate Programs offer various market types for affiliates to earn commissions. CPA (Cost per Acquisition) pays affiliates a fixed amount when referred traders make a qualifying deposit or complete a specific action. CPL (Cost per Lead) pays affiliates a fixed amount for each lead generated, regardless of whether they convert into traders or not. Revenue Share pays affiliates a percentage of the revenue generated by referred traders, ensuring long-term earnings. These market types provide different ways for affiliates to earn money based on the specific actions and outcomes of their referred traders.</p></p>
