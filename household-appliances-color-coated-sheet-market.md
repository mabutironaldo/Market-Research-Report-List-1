<p><p>The Household Appliances Color-Coated Sheet market includes two types: PCM (Pre-Coated Metal) and VCM (Vinyl Coated Metal). PCM sheets are metal sheets coated with a special high-temperature baking paint, which provides various colors and patterns. These sheets are primarily used for kitchen appliances like refrigerators and ovens. VCM sheets, on the other hand, are metal sheets coated with a layer of vinyl film. They offer a wider range of colors and patterns and are commonly used in various household appliances such as washing machines and air conditioners.</p></p>
