<p><strong>The Automotive Air Spring Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Capsule Air Spring</li><li>Membrane Air Spring</li><li>Compound Air Spring</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive air spring market consists of three types: capsule air spring, membrane air spring, and compound air spring. A capsule air spring utilizes a rubber bellows enclosed in a steel or aluminum capsule to provide suspension support and reduce vibrations. A membrane air spring uses a rubber membrane to separate the air and support the suspension system. On the other hand, a compound air spring combines the features of both capsule and membrane air springs to offer improved durability, stability, and load-carrying capacity in automotive applications.</p></p>
