<p><p>The household hot water storage tank market can be divided into two main types: electric storage and fuel storage. Electric storage tanks use electricity to heat the water and store it for future use, while fuel storage tanks rely on fuels like gas or oil for heating. Both types serve the purpose of providing hot water to households, but differ in terms of energy source. Electric storage tanks are commonly used in areas with access to electricity, while fuel storage tanks are preferred in regions with limited or no electric supply.</p></p>
