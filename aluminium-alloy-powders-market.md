<p><p>The Aluminium Alloy Powders market can be classified into three types: Fine Aluminium Alloy Powders, Medium Aluminium Alloy Powders, and Coarse Aluminium Alloy Powders. Fine powders have smaller particle size and are commonly used for applications requiring high surface area and good flowability. Medium powders have intermediate particle size and are utilized in applications that require a balance between surface area and flowability. Coarse powders have larger particle size and are suitable for applications that prioritize good flowability while sacrificing surface area.</p></p>
