Coffee vending machines are designed to dispense pre-packaged coffee, providing convenience in public spaces. Filter coffee machines brew coffee by pouring hot water over ground coffee beans, suitable for large-scale consumption, like in offices. Espresso machines are engineered to brew coffee quickly by forcing hot water through finely ground coffee, producing a strong and concentrated flavor, ideal for coffee enthusiasts or commercial establishments.</p></p>
