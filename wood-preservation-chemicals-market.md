<p><p>Wood preservation chemicals are used to protect and enhance the durability of wood products. Water-borne preservatives are chemical formulations that are diluted with water before application, providing a cost-effective and environmentally friendly option. Oil-borne preservatives are oil-based formulations that penetrate deep into the wood, offering excellent weather resistance. Light organic solvent preservatives are solvent-based formulations that contain organic solvents, which allow easy penetration and distribution within the wood. These three types cater to different application needs and provide effective protection against decay, insects, and fungi.</p></p>
