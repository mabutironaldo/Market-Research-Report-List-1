<p><p>Air Care Aerosol Market Types - Two-Phase Aerosol, Three-Phase Aerosol:</p><p>Two-Phase Aerosol refers to a type of air care aerosol that contains only a liquid phase and a gas phase. The liquid phase often contains the fragrance or active ingredients, while the gas phase acts as the propellant. This type is commonly used for products like air fresheners.</p><p>On the other hand, Three-Phase Aerosol refers to a type of air care aerosol that contains three distinct phases - liquid, solid, and gas. The solid phase is usually a suspended powder, while the liquid phase consists of fragrance or active ingredients. The gas phase acts as the propellant. This type is often used for products like foam-type air fresheners.</p></p>
