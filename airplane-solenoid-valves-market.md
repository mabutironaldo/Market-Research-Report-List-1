<p><p>The Airplane Solenoid Valves market comprises two types: 2 Way Airplane Solenoid Valves and 3 Way Airplane Solenoid Valves. 2 Way Valves are designed to control the flow of fluids in two directions, allowing for efficient on/off control. They find application in tasks like fuel control and hydraulic systems. On the other hand, 3 Way Valves are capable of diverting the flow of fluids, making them suitable for functions like fluid mixing or routing. These valves are commonly used in aircraft systems for functions such as air conditioning and pneumatic control.</p></p>
