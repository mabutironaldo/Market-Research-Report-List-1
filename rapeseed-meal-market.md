<p><p>Rapeseed meal, also known as canola meal, is a byproduct obtained during the process of extracting oil from rapeseed (Brassica napus), a flowering plant of the cruciferous family. Rapeseed meal offers several nutritional benefits, including high protein content, essential amino acids, and energy density, making it a popular choice among animal nutritionists.</p><p>Moving on to the market analysis and latest trends, the rapeseed meal market is expected to witness significant growth in the coming years.
