<p><p>Cosmetic grade kaolin is a natural mineral used in various cosmetic applications. There are three main types - hard kaolin, soft kaolin, and sandy kaolin, each offering distinct characteristics. Hard kaolin has a high composition of kaolinite, making it have a denser structure that provides excellent oil absorption and smooth texture. Soft kaolin contains a lower proportion of kaolinite, making it more gentle and suitable for sensitive skin. Sandy kaolin, on the other hand, has a coarser texture, ideal for exfoliating properties in cosmetic formulations. These varying types cater to different cosmetic needs and preferences.</p></p>
