<p><p>The waterproof breathable textiles market can be categorized into three types: densely woven, coated, and membrane. Densely woven textiles are made by tightly weaving the fabric, forming a barrier against water penetration. Coated textiles have a layer of waterproof coating applied to the fabric's surface, preventing water ingress. Membrane textiles include a thin waterproof membrane sandwiched between layers of fabric, allowing moisture to escape while blocking liquid water. Each type offers different advantages and applications, giving consumers choices in selecting the most suitable waterproof breathable textile for their needs.</p></p>
