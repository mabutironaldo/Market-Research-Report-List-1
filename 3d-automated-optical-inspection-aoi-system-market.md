<p><p>The 3D Automated Optical Inspection (AOI) System market consists of two types: Inline AOI and Offline AOI. The Inline AOI market involves the integration of inspecting systems directly into the production line, allowing real-time defect detection and immediate action. It offers higher efficiency and reduced production cycle time, making it suitable for high-volume manufacturing. On the other hand, Offline AOI is a standalone inspection system that operates separately from the production line. It provides flexibility for small batch and prototype production, offering detailed analysis and thorough inspection at a slower pace.</p></p>
