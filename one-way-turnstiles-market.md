<p><p>One-way turnstiles are security gates used to control the flow of people in a specific direction. There are two main types: half-height turnstiles and full-height turnstiles. Half-height turnstiles are waist-high gates that allow for easy passage and are commonly found in subway stations, stadiums, and office buildings. Both types offer efficient and controlled access and are designed to enhance safety and prevent unauthorized entry.</p></p>
