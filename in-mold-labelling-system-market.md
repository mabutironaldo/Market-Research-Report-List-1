<p><p>The In-Mold Labelling System market comprises various types based on different molding processes. Injection molding involves injecting molten plastic into a mold cavity, resulting in the desired shape of the product. Blow molding is a process where air pressure is used to inflate the molten plastic into a hollow object. Thermoforming involves heating a plastic sheet and shaping it by using a mold. These processes are utilized in the In-Mold Labelling System market to efficiently apply labels during the molding process, resulting in a seamless and durable product.</p></p>
