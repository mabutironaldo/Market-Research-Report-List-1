<p><p>The automotive green glass market includes two types: infra-red cut and ultraviolet cut. Infra-red cut glass is designed to reduce the amount of heat entering the vehicle, providing a cooler interior. This type of glass helps to block the infrared rays from the sun, reducing the need for excessive air conditioning. On the other hand, ultraviolet cut glass is specifically engineered to block harmful ultraviolet (UV) rays. This protects both passengers and the vehicle's interior from UV radiation, preventing damage to skin and materials like upholstery and dashboard. Both types contribute to a safer and more comfortable driving experience.</p></p>
