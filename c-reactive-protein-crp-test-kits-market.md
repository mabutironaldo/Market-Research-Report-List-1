<p><p>The C-Reactive Protein (CRP) Test Kits market can be classified into different types based on the technology used for detection. These types include immunoturbidimetric, ELISA (enzyme-linked immunosorbent assay), chemiluminescent immunoassay (CLIA), and others. Immunoturbidimetric test kits utilize the principle of measuring the turbidity produced when antigen-antibody complexes form. ELISA kits involve the use of antibodies linked to enzymes for detecting CRP levels. CLIA kits use chemiluminescent substances to generate light signals that indicate the presence of CRP. Other types of kits may employ different methodologies for CRP detection.</p></p>
