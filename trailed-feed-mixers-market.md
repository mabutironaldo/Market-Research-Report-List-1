<p><ul><li>Vertical</li><li>Horizontal</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Trailed feed mixers are categorized into two types based on their orientation in the market: vertical and horizontal. Vertical trailed feed mixers have a vertical mixing chamber where the feed ingredients are blended using a vertical auger. This type is known for its efficient mixing and ability to handle a wide range of ingredients. On the other hand, horizontal trailed feed mixers have a horizontal mixing chamber with one or two augers. This type is favored for its faster mixing time and ease of unloading. Both types offer unique advantages for farmers and feed production facilities.</p></p>
