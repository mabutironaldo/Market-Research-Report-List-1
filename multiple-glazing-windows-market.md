<p><p>Multiple glazing windows refer to windows that are constructed with multiple layers of glass, with space in between them filled with air or gas. These windows offer various benefits such as energy efficiency, noise reduction, and enhanced security. Different types of multiple glazing windows include heat absorbing, insulated, low-emissivity coatings, reflective coatings, spectrally selective coating, and others. Heat absorbing windows are designed to absorb and radiate heat, while insulated windows provide better thermal insulation. Low-emissivity coatings reduce heat transfer, reflective coatings reflect heat and block UV rays, and spectrally selective coatings allow in specific wavelengths of light while blocking others. Other types may include self-cleaning windows or laminated glass for improved safety.</p></p>
